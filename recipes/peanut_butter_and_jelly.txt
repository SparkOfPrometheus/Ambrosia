Title: Peanut Butter and Jelly
Description: A sandwich with peanut butter and jelly
Tags: lunch
Ingredients:
- Peanut_Butter
- Jelly
- Sandwich_Bread
Steps:
1. 1. Place two pieces of bread on a cutting board
2. 2. Spread peanut butter on one piece
3. 3. Spread Jelly on the other piece
4. 4. Press peanut butter and jelly together
5. 5. Enjoy
