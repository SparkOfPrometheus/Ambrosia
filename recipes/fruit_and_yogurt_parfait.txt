Title: Fruit and Yogurt Parfait
Description: A layered treat with yogurt, fruit, and granola.
Tags: breakfast, dessert
Ingredients:
- Fruit
- Yogurt
- Granola
Steps:
1. 1. In a glass or bowl, spoon a layer of yogurt.
2. 2. Add a layer of fresh fruit.
3. 3. Sprinkle a layer of granola on top.
4. 4. Repeat layers until the container is full.
5. 5. Enjoy!
