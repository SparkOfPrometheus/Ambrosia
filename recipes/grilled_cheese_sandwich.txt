Title: Grilled Cheese Sandwich
Description: A classic sandwich with gooey melted cheese.
Tags: lunch
Ingredients:
- Bread
- Cheddar_cheese
- Butter
Steps:
1. 1. Heat a skillet on medium heat.
2. 2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
3. 3. Place one slice, buttered side down, in the skillet.
4. 4. Add cheese slices on top.
5. 5. Place the second slice of bread, buttered side up, over the cheese.
6. 6. Cook until golden brown on one side, then flip and cook the other side.
7. 7. Enjoy!
